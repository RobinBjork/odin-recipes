Lasagna

Description for this recipe

This recipe will be for a delightful lasagna bolognese

This is the perfect recipe for making the weekly lunch box

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Mozzarella
  • Yellow onion
  • Lasagna plates
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Step by step

  1. Make beef sauce
  2. Put on the oven
  3. Build lasagna with cheese, sauce and plates
  4. Cook for 35min
  5. Enjoy!
