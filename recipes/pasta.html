Spagetti

Description for this recipe

This recipe will be for a heavenly and oily pasta

Cook it with love and this simple recipe will fill you with joy!

Ingredients

  • Good quality spagetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Chili
  • Parsely
  • Salt and pepper

Step by step

  1. Cook the paste until it's almost finished
  2. Chop and fry garlic in lot's of olive oil
  3. Put in chili, salt and pepper
  4. Put in some pasta water
  5. Mix everything together
  6. Top with more pepper and parsley
  7. Enjoy!
