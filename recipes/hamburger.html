Hamburger

Description for this recipe

This recipe will be for a delightful smashburger made with love and lots of trial and error

It is best cooked using a stainless steel or cast iron pan, or even better a big flat-top

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • American cheese
  • Red onion
  • Sallad
  • Hamburger bread
  • Salt and pepper
  • Which ever sauces you enjoy

Step by step

  1. Take out the beef from the fridge 20 min before cooking
  2. Roll the beef into 2 round balls, 125g each
  3. Dice the sallad and onions
  4. Put on your pan on high temp and let it get really hot
  5. Put your beef balls in the pan and smash them down, do not touch them until it's time to flip
  6. Put salt and pepper on the smashed down beef
  7. After a few minutes, flip once and put the cheese on the pattys
  8. Put the pattys on top of each other
  9. Heaten the bread
  10. Build the burger
  11. Enjoy!
